Introducing Our New Robot — The uFactory xArm6

Huge news for our team: we've officially added the UFactory xArm6 to our robotics lineup! This 6-axis collaborative robotic arm is a major milestone for our club and opens the door to an entirely new dimension of projects.

The xArm6 is designed for precision, flexibility, and ease of use. With a 5 kg payload, 0.1 mm repeatability, and a reach of 700 mm, it's capable of performing an impressive range of tasks—from pick-and-place operations to advanced motion planning experiments. Its open-source-friendly ecosystem also means we can integrate it with Python, ROS, machine vision systems, and custom tooling.

Whether we're experimenting with automation, training newcomers on industrial workflows, or pushing the limits of what we can simulate using robotics algorithms, the xArm6 gives us a powerful platform to innovate.
