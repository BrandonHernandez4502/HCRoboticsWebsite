Our Mission

The Holy Cross Robotics Club is dedicated to giving STEM students a hands-on outlet to express their creativity, curiosity, and technical skills. With the support of the Math and Computer Science Department, we provide students with opportunities to start meaningful projects, experiment with real hardware, and explore the world of robotics from the ground up. Our mission is to create an environment where students can learn essential tools—from programming and electronics to robotics concepts and collaborative workflows—while building innovative projects that challenge and inspire them. Through mentorship, teamwork, and a passion for discovery, we empower members to grow as engineers, creators, and problem-solvers.
